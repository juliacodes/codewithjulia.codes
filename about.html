CodeWithJulia.codes

I started this site, because I noticed something missing when I was learning web development.
There are a ridiculously large amount of online resources for learning front end and back end, but these sites are either extremely confusing to read, or extremely displeasing to look at.

My goal was simple, Create a resource for beginners and advanced programmers that is:

  • Easy to Read
  • Straight forward
  • & Well Styled

Pages I've found display examples for buttons,forms, etc, but they look dated and don't utilize modern techniques.
